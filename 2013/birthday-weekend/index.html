Birthday weekend

I had a great weekend; Mom and Dad visited.

On Saturday, we went out to a late lunch, and later that evening we played ping pong while watching college basketball and I cooked dinner for them.

On Sunday, Akina joined us for lunch. After the parents flew back in the afternoon, Akina and I hung out and had a belated birthday dinner at Sura, a Korean barbecue joint in Oakland.
