Mon, 30 Dec 2013

Winter break

It’s been a good break so far. I got to make some meals for Mom and Jen, such as slow-roasted pork shoulder two ways (one in garlic oil, one in tomato sauce), slow-roasted chicken thighs, frittata, and oxtail stew. And on Christmas, we went over to the Wanningers for dinner (ham, green beans, and salads).

I got to see some friends, too: Souplantation with Kevin, Tiffany, and Byron; basketball at Gallup with the usual crew; and Josh came over a couple days ago. Yesterday, Akina drove down to visit and we went to the beach and ate Wingstop. Later, we met up with Chris to see Her, the new movie by Spike Jonze.
